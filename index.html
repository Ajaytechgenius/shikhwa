Can a student really teach better than an experienced teacher? +

Being closer to the exam and the confusion myself means I explain things the way I actually needed them explained — often simpler and more relatable than a textbook approach.

Just message me in advance where possible, and we'll reschedule. Life happens — the goal is consistent learning, not rigid rules.

Do you cover both school exams and JEE/NEET together? +

Yes — Physics concepts overlap significantly between board exams and competitive exams. We can focus on one or balance both depending on your goals.

Is the free trial class really free? +

Yes, completely free with no obligation. It's the best way for you to see if the teaching style works for you before committing.

Can I get doubt support outside of class hours? +

Yes, reasonable doubt-clearing over WhatsApp is included — I know how important that is, especially close to exams.
